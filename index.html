Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29.06.2026

Organization: Raqamli Nazorat

1. General information

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") provides information on how the information of users of the "M-Gaz" program ("You" or "User") created and managed by the Raqamli Nazorat organization is collected, stored and protected. By using the program, you agree to the terms of this Policy.

2. User scope

This program is intended only for employees of the organization. External users (general public) do not have the right to use the program. Each user is created a login and password by the organization to log in to the system. The user cannot change this login information and access to the system is only possible based on the permission granted by the organization.

3. Collected data

The program stores the following data:

  • Region
  • District
  • Name and surname
  • Phone number
  • Date of birth
  • Geographic location (GPS) — see section 5

This data is intended for internal use of the organization only and will not be provided to third parties without the user's permission.

4. Purpose of using data

  • Maintaining a list of employees within the organization;
  • Identifying users;
  • Automating the organization's activities;
  • Maintaining an internal reporting and control system.

5. Location data

The M-Gaz application collects employees' geographic location (GPS) data. This data is processed solely for work purposes and only in connection with the employee's performance of work duties. The application is a corporate (work) tool and does not collect location for any personal, advertising or marketing purpose.

Location data is collected for the following purposes:

  • Monitoring employees' work locations on a map during working hours;
  • Determining and confirming the location where field tasks (measurements, inspections, document collection, attendance check-in, etc.) are performed;
  • Recording the place where work-related activities are carried out;
  • Coordinating and managing the organization's field operations.

Location data may be collected while the application is in use and, where it is required for the work purposes listed above, while the application runs in the background during working hours. Before location collection begins, the application clearly informs the employee and requests their consent together with the corresponding device permission. The employee can disable location access at any time through the device settings; however, this may limit the work-related functions of the application.

Location data is intended exclusively for the internal use of the organization. It is not sold, transferred, rented or otherwise disclosed to any third parties, and is not used for advertising or any purpose unrelated to the employee's work. The data is stored on secure servers and is accessible only to authorized employees of the organization.

6. Privacy and security

We take all necessary measures to protect users' personal data. The data is stored on secure servers and can only be viewed by authorized employees.

Apart from the location data described in section 5 above, we do not collect, store or use users' IP address, computer or other device information for any purpose unrelated to work.

7. Sharing information with third parties

We do not sell or share users' personal information — including location data — with third parties under any circumstances.

8. Cookies and tracking

The application may use cookies. You can manage or delete cookie settings on your device. Cookies are used to store user preferences.

9. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Users will be notified if significant changes are made.

10. Contact

Maxfiylik siyosati

Oxirgi yangilanish sanasi: 29.06.2026

Tashkilot: Raqamli Nazorat

1. Umumiy ma'lumot

Ushbu Maxfiylik siyosati ("Siyosat") Raqamli Nazorat tashkiloti tomonidan yaratilgan va boshqariladigan "M-Gaz" dasturi foydalanuvchilari ("Siz" yoki "Foydalanuvchi")ning ma'lumotlarini qanday yig'ilishi, saqlanishi va himoya qilinishi haqida ma'lumot beradi. Dasturdan foydalanish orqali Siz ushbu Siyosatdagi shartlarga rozilik bildirgan hisoblanasiz.

2. Foydalanuvchilar doirasi

Ushbu dastur faqat tashkiloti xodimlari uchun mo'ljallangan. Dasturdan tashqi foydalanuvchilar (oddiy jamoatchilik) foydalanish huquqiga ega emas. Har bir foydalanuvchiga tizimga kirish uchun tashkilot tomonidan login va parol yaratiladi. Foydalanuvchi ushbu login ma'lumotlarini o'zgartira olmaydi va tizimga kirish faqat tashkilot tomonidan berilgan ruxsat asosida amalga oshiriladi.

3. Yig'iladigan ma'lumotlar

Dastur quyidagi ma'lumotlarni saqlaydi:

  • Viloyat
  • Tuman
  • Ism va familiya
  • Telefon raqami
  • Tug'ilgan sana
  • Geografik joylashuv (GPS) — 5-bo'limga qarang

Ushbu ma'lumotlar faqat tashkilot ichki foydalanishi uchun mo'ljallangan bo'lib, foydalanuvchining ruxsatisiz uchinchi tomonlarga berilmaydi.

4. Ma'lumotlardan foydalanish maqsadi

  • Tashkilot ichidagi xodimlar ro'yxatini yuritish;
  • Foydalanuvchilarni identifikatsiya qilish;
  • Tashkilot faoliyatini avtomatlashtirish;
  • Ichki hisobot va nazorat tizimini yuritish.

5. Joylashuv ma'lumotlari

M-Gaz ilovasi xodimlarning geografik joylashuv (GPS) ma'lumotlarini yig'adi. Ushbu ma'lumotlar faqat ish maqsadlarida va faqat xodimning ish vazifalarini bajarishi bilan bog'liq holda qayta ishlanadi. Ilova korporativ (ishga oid) vosita bo'lib, joylashuvni hech qanday shaxsiy, reklama yoki marketing maqsadida yig'maydi.

Joylashuv ma'lumotlari quyidagi maqsadlarda yig'iladi:

  • Ish vaqtida xodimlarning ish joylarini xaritada kuzatish;
  • Dala vazifalari (o'lchovlar, tekshiruvlar, hujjatlarni yig'ish, davomatni belgilash va boshqalar) bajarilgan joyni aniqlash va tasdiqlash;
  • Ishga oid harakatlar amalga oshirilgan joyni qayd etish;
  • Tashkilotning dala faoliyatini muvofiqlashtirish va boshqarish.

Joylashuv ma'lumotlari ilovadan foydalanilayotgan vaqtda, shuningdek yuqorida sanab o'tilgan ish maqsadlari uchun zarur bo'lganda, ish vaqti davomida ilova fonda (background) ishlayotganda ham yig'ilishi mumkin. Joylashuvni yig'ish boshlanishidan oldin ilova xodimni aniq xabardor qiladi va uning rozaligini hamda qurilmaning tegishli ruxsatini so'raydi. Xodim istalgan vaqtda qurilma sozlamalari orqali joylashuvga ruxsatni o'chirib qo'yishi mumkin; biroq bu ilovaning ishga oid funksiyalarini cheklashi mumkin.

Joylashuv ma'lumotlari faqat tashkilotning ichki foydalanishi uchun mo'ljallangan. Ular hech qanday holatda uchinchi shaxslarga sotilmaydi, berilmaydi, ijaraga berilmaydi yoki oshkor qilinmaydi va reklama yoki xodimning ishiga aloqasi bo'lmagan hech qanday maqsadda ishlatilmaydi. Ma'lumotlar xavfsiz serverlarda saqlanadi va faqat tashkilotning ruxsat berilgan xodimlari uchun ochiq bo'ladi.

6. Maxfiylik va xavfsizlik

Biz foydalanuvchilarning shaxsiy ma'lumotlarini himoya qilish uchun barcha zarur choralarni ko'ramiz. Ma'lumotlar xavfsiz serverlarda saqlanadi va faqat ruxsat berilgan xodimlar tomonidan ko'rilishi mumkin.

Yuqoridagi 5-bo'limda tavsiflangan joylashuv ma'lumotlaridan tashqari, biz foydalanuvchilarning IP manzili, kompyuter yoki boshqa qurilma ma'lumotlarini ishga aloqasi bo'lmagan hech qanday maqsadda yig'maymiz, saqlamaymiz va ishlatmaymiz.

7. Ma'lumotlarni uchinchi shaxslarga berish

Biz foydalanuvchilarning shaxsiy ma'lumotlarini — joylashuv ma'lumotlari ham — hech qanday holatda uchinchi tomonlarga sotmaymiz yoki ulashmaymiz.

8. Cookie fayllari va kuzatish

Ilova cookie-fayllardan foydalanishi mumkin. Siz cookie sozlamalarini o'zingizning qurilmangizda boshqarishingiz yoki o'chirishingiz mumkin. Cookie'lar foydalanuvchi afzalliklarini saqlash uchun ishlatiladi.

9. O'zgarishlar

Ushbu Maxfiylik siyosati vaqti-vaqti bilan yangilanishi mumkin. Agar muhim o'zgarishlar kiritilsa, foydalanuvchilarga bu haqda xabar beriladi.
